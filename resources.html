Course Syllabus

Insight into the core concepts and areas of study in this course

General Circulation of Atmosphere

  • Understanding the large-scale movements of air
  • Exploring wind patterns and atmospheric pressure systems
  • Impact of the rotation of the Earth on the atmosphere

Indian Monsoon System

  • Understanding the Indian Monsoon System
  • Seasonal changes in wind patterns and precipitation

Extreme Weather/Climate Index

  • Exploring the Extreme Weather/Climate Index
  • Understanding how it's used to monitor and measure the frequency, duration, and intensity of extreme weather and climate events
